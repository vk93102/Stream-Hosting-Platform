OBS VM Setup Guide

Once your VM is Running:

  1. Click Open noVNC → to access OBS in your browser
  2. OBS is pre-configured to stream via SRT → SIL ingest → your destinations
  3. Add your scenes and sources in OBS normally
  4. Hit Start Streaming in OBS – SIL picks it up automatically
  5. To control OBS remotely via API: use the OBS WebSocket plugin on port 4455
